How to use it

Four beginner questions that clear the fog.

When a chapter feels confusing, do not try to solve everything at once. Ask one category question and move forward.

1

What word matters?

Look up the one term that keeps repeating, then reread the sentence.

2

Who is speaking?

Notice whether Jesus, a disciple, a crowd, or a religious leader is driving the scene.

3

Where are they?

Galilee, Jerusalem, Samaria, and the Temple each carry different tensions.

4

When is this?

Place the moment before or after the cross, resurrection, and early church expansion.
